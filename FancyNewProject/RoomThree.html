Victory

As you drop the last disk in place a click is heard, and with a low rumble the door begins to slowly slide open.
When you enter the room you notice something strange. The door at the end of the room is wide open. a stone plaque sits alone in this empty room. The plaque reads

“I couldn’t think of a good third riddle. The treasure is yours, just tell anyone that ask that the third riddle was really clever and hard, Thanks. I just spent way to much time trying to paint the disk in the last room I thought two riddles was enough.”


You walk into the final room to see the treasure you spent years looking for. You celebrate your victory and think about resetting the watch to do it all over again.
